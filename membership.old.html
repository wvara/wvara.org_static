Membership

Membership is available to all persons interested in Amateur radio. There is no screening process or sponsorship required to become a member. To become a member, contact the Association Treasurer, Jon Kelley K6WV. We encourage prospective members to sign up for membership at our meetings. Membership related correspondence and renewals can be mailed to the Association mailbox: P.O. Box 6544, San Jose, CA 95150-6544.

ARRL Membership

If you plan to join the American Radio Relay League (ARRL) WVARA receives a rebate for sending the application form and fee to headquarters. Please send your application form and fee to: WVARA Treasurer P.O. Box 6544 San Jose, Ca 95150-6544. Make your check out to WVARA for the amount specified by the ARRL. WVARA will forward the appllication form and a WVARA check for the fee less the rebate. It doesn't cost you any more this way, but it does provide additional support for WVARA to continue providing Amateur radio activities for the community.
