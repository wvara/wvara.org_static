Regular Monthly Meetings

WVARA meetings are on the second Wednesday of each month except July, August, and December. The meetings start at 7:00 pm. The complete meeting schedule is on our Home Page.

Visitors and guests are welcome to attend the meetings, and pre-meeting gatherings!

Directions to the Regular Meetings

We meet at the Santa Clara Valley Chapter of the American Red Cross, at 2731 North 1st Street in San Jose. See the map for the American Red Cross.

Pre-meeting Unoffical Gatherings: For Location and time, please contact Jim K6EI using the email address on his QRZ.com page.

If you have a suggestion for a meeting topic, please contact our association's vice president.
